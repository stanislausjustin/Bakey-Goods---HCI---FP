Ingredients:


- 1 cup old-fashioned oats
- 1 ripe banana
- 1/2 cup milk (dairy or non-dairy)
- 1 large egg
- 1 tablespoon honey or maple syrup
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 1/4 teaspoon cinnamon (optional)
- Pinch of salt
- Cooking oil or butter for the pan

Step by steps


1. Blend Ingredients:

- In a blender or food processor, combine oats, banana, milk, egg, honey or maple syrup, baking powder, vanilla extract, cinnamon (if using), and a pinch of salt.
- Blend until you have a smooth batter.


2. Preheat Griddle or Pan:

- Preheat a griddle or non-stick pan over medium heat. Add a little oil or butter to coat the surface.


3. Cook Pancakes:

- Pour 1/4 cup of batter onto the griddle for each pancake.
- Cook until bubbles form on the surface and the edges look set, then flip and cook until the other side is golden brown.


4. Serve:

- Repeat until all the batter is used.
- Serve the pancakes warm with your favorite toppings, such as sliced bananas, berries, or a drizzle of maple syrup.
