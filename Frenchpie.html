French Silk Pie


Indulge in the velvety bliss of our French Silk Pie. Silky smooth chocolate meets clouds of whipped cream in a buttery, flaky crust, creating
a decadent symphony of flavors. A slice of pure luxury, each bite is a journey into chocolate heaven.





Ingredients:


- 1 1/2 cups chocolate cookie crumbs
- 1/3 cup unsalted butter, melted
- 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
- 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
- 4 ounces unsweetened chocolate, melted and cooled
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- 4 large eggs
- 1 cup heavy cream
- 2 tablespoons powdered sugar
- 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Spinona Djunayde


Spinona's French Silk Pie is an indulgent masterpiece, marrying the richness of silky chocolate with clouds of whipped cream, all cradled in a buttery, flaky crust. It's a heavenly symphony of decadent flavors, offering pure bliss in every luxurious bite.





Step by steps


1. For the crust, combine chocolate cookie crumbs and melted butter. Press into a pie dish and chill.

2. For the filling, beat softened butter and sugar until light and fluffy. Add melted chocolate and vanilla extract. Beat until smooth.

3. Add eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Continue beating for 5 minutes until the mixture is light and fluffy.

4. Pour the filling into the chilled crust and refrigerate for at least 4 hours or until set.

5. For the whipped cream topping, whip heavy cream, powdered sugar, and vanilla extract until stiff peaks form. Pipe or spread over the pie before serving.

6. Chill until ready to serve.
