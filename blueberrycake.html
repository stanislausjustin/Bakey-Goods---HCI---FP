Lemon blueberry layer cake


Indulge in a symphony of summer flavors with our Blueberry Lemon Layer Cake. Each bite is a burst of
sunshine, where zesty lemon layers dance with luscious blueberry filling. A perfect harmony of sweet and tangy, crowned with a velvety frosting.
Elevate your dessert experience and savor the essence of summer in every slice!





Ingredients:


- 2 and 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
- 1 and 1/2 cups granulated sugar
- 4 large eggs
- Zest of 2 lemons
- 1/4 cup fresh lemon juice
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 1 cup buttermilk
- 1 and 1/2 cups fresh blueberries


For the lemon glaze:

- 1 cup powdered sugar
- 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
- Zest of 1 lemon

Indulge in Spinona's Lemon Blueberry Layer Cake. A burst of zesty lemon, luscious blueberry, and the perfect flavor harmony. Make your next celebration unforgettable with this tantalizing treat!





Step by steps


1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour a 9-inch round cake pan.

2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Set aside.

3. In a large mixing bowl, cream together the softened butter and granulated sugar until light and fluffy.

4. Beat in the eggs, one at a time, followed by the lemon zest, lemon juice, and vanilla extract.

5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, alternating with the buttermilk. Begin and end with the dry ingredients, mixing until just combined.

6. Gently fold in the blueberries.

7. Pour the batter into the prepared cake pan and smooth the top with a spatula.

8. Bake for 35-40 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

9. Allow the cake to cool in the pan for 10 minutes, then transfer it to a wire rack to cool completely.

10. In a small bowl, whisk together the powdered sugar, lemon juice, and lemon zest to make the lemon glaze. Drizzle the glaze over the cooled cake.

11. Slice and serve your delicious lemon blueberry cake!
