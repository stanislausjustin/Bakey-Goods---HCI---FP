Classic Lemon Meringue Pie


Experience the timeless delight of our Classic Lemon Meringue Pie. Zesty lemon curd meets fluffy meringue atop a golden crust, creating a
perfect harmony of sweet and tangy flavors. A slice of pure nostalgia, bringing joy with every bite.





Ingredients:


- 1 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
- 1/2 cup unsalted butter, cold and cubed
- 1/4 cup granulated sugar
- 1/4 cup cornstarch
- 3-4 tablespoons ice water
- 1 cup granulated sugar
- 3 tablespoons cornstarch
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- 1 1/2 cups water
- 3 large egg yolks, beaten
- 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
- 1/2 cup fresh lemon juice
- Zest of one lemon
- 3 large egg whites
- 1/4 teaspoon cream of tartar
- 6 tablespoons granulated sugar

Spinona Djunayde


Spinona's Classic Lemon Meringue Pie is a symphony of sweet and tangy perfection. The velvety lemon filling, bursting with zesty goodness, is nestled in a golden, flaky crust that elevates each heavenly bite. A timeless dessert that captures the essence of citrus-infused bliss.





Step by steps


1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).

2. For the crust, combine flour, butter, and sugar in a food processor. Add ice water gradually until the dough comes together. Press into a pie dish and bake for 12-15 minutes, or until golden brown.

3. For the lemon filling, whisk together sugar, cornstarch, and salt in a saucepan. Gradually add water, stirring until smooth. Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly until the mixture thickens.

4. Remove from heat, and gradually whisk in beaten egg yolks. Return to heat and cook for an additional 2 minutes.

5. Remove from heat and stir in butter, lemon juice, and zest. Pour into the pre-baked pie crust.

6. For the meringue, beat egg whites and cream of tartar until soft peaks form. Gradually add sugar and continue beating until stiff peaks form. Spread over the lemon filling.

7. Bake for 10-12 minutes or until the meringue is golden brown. Cool before serving.
