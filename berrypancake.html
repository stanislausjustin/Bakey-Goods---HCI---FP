Berry Fluffy Pancakes


Rise and shine to a burst of berry bliss with our Berry Fluffy Pancakes! Each bite is a cloud of perfection, blending plump, juicy berries into a symphony
of flavor. Elevate your breakfast game and start your day on a fluffy, fruity high note!!





Ingredients:


- 1 cup all-purpose flour
- 2 tablespoons granulated sugar
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- 3/4 cup buttermilk
- 1 large egg
- 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 1 cup mixed berries (such as blueberries, raspberries, and strawberries)

Spinona Djunayde


Spinona's Berry Fluffy Pancakes are a breakfast sensation! Picture this: clouds of fluffy goodness infused with plump, juicy berries, creating a burst of fruity perfection in every bite. It's a delightful morning treat that elevates breakfast to a whole new level, ensuring your day starts on the sweetest note!





Step by steps


1. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.

2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the buttermilk, egg, melted butter, and vanilla extract.

3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined. Don't overmix; it's okay if there are some lumps.

4. Gently fold in the mixed berries.

5. Heat a griddle or non-stick skillet over medium heat and lightly grease with butter or cooking spray.

6. Pour 1/4 cup of batter for each pancake onto the griddle.

7. Cook until bubbles form on the surface of the pancake and the edges start to look set, then flip and cook until golden brown on the other side.

8. Repeat with the remaining batter.

9. Serve the berry fluffy pancakes warm with maple syrup, additional berries, or your favorite toppings.



Start your day on a sweet note, and let the joy of juicy berries elevate your mornings. Happy cooking and even happier eating!
