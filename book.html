Double chocolate cake

Ingredients:

For the Cake:

1 and 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
2 cups granulated sugar
1 and 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
1 and 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
1 teaspoon salt
2 large eggs
1 cup whole milk
1/2 cup vegetable oil
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
1 cup boiling water

For the Chocolate Ganache:

1 cup semisweet chocolate chips
1/2 cup heavy cream

Instructions:

For the Cake:

1. Preheat your oven to 350째F (175째C). Grease and flour two 9-inch round cake pans.
2. In a large mixing bowl, sift together the flour, cocoa powder, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
3. Add the eggs, milk, vegetable oil, and vanilla extract to the dry ingredients. Mix on medium speed until well combined.
4. Reduce the speed to low and gradually add the boiling water to the batter. Mix until smooth. The batter will be thin, but that's okay.
5. Pour the batter evenly into the prepared cake pans.
6. Bake in the preheated oven for 30 to 35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
7. Allow the cakes to cool in the pans for 10 minutes, then transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely.

For the Chocolate Ganache:

1. In a small saucepan, heat the heavy cream over medium heat until it just begins to simmer. Be careful not to let it boil.
2. Place the chocolate chips in a heatproof bowl. Pour the hot cream over the chocolate and let it sit for 2 minutes.
3. Stir the chocolate and cream together until smooth and glossy.
4. Let the ganache cool for a few minutes until it thickens slightly.

Assembling the Cake:

1. Once the cakes are completely cool, place one layer on a serving plate. Spread a layer of chocolate ganache over the top.
2. Place the second layer on top and cover the entire cake with the remaining chocolate ganache.
3. You can also decorate the cake with chocolate shavings, sprinkles, or any other toppings of your choice.
4. Allow the ganache to set before slicing and serving.
crunchy chocolate chip cookies:

Ingredients:

1 cup (2 sticks) unsalted butter, softened
1 cup granulated sugar
2 cups all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 large egg
2 cups chocolate chips

Instructions:

1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 350째F (175째C). Line baking sheets with parchment paper.
2. Creaming Butter and Sugar: In a bowl, cream together the softened butter and granulated sugar until smooth.
3. Adding Egg and Vanilla: Beat in the egg and vanilla extract until well combined.
4. Dry Ingredients: In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, and salt.
5. Combining Wet and Dry Ingredients: Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined.
6. Adding Chocolate Chips: Fold in the chocolate chips.
7. Forming Cookies: Drop rounded tablespoons of dough onto the prepared baking sheets, spacing them about 2 inches apart.
8. Baking: Bake in the preheated oven for 10-12 minutes or until the edges are golden brown.
9. Cooling: Allow the cookies to cool on the baking sheets for a few minutes before transferring them to wire racks to cool completely.
10. Enjoy: Once completely cooled, enjoy your simple and crunchy chocolate chip cookies!
Fluffy Berry Pancakes::

Ingredients:

1 cup all-purpose flour
2 tablespoons sugar
1 teaspoon baking powder
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
1/4 teaspoon salt
3/4 cup buttermilk
1/4 cup milk
1 large egg
2 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 cup mixed berries (blueberries, raspberries, strawberries)

Instructions:

1. Preheat Griddle or Pan: Preheat a griddle or non-stick skillet over medium heat.
2. Mix Dry Ingredients: In a mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
3. Combine Wet Ingredients: In a separate bowl, whisk together the buttermilk, milk, egg, melted butter, and vanilla extract.
4. Combine Wet and Dry Ingredients: Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined. The batter may be a bit lumpy, but avoid overmixing.
5. Add Berries: Gently fold in the mixed berries into the batter.
6. Grease Griddle or Pan: Lightly grease the griddle or pan with butter or cooking spray.
7. Scoop Batter: Using a ladle or measuring cup, pour 1/4 cup of batter for each pancake onto the griddle.
8. Cook Pancakes: Cook until bubbles form on the surface of the pancakes and the edges start to look set, then flip and cook until the other side is golden brown.
9. Repeat: Continue until all the batter is used, adding more butter or cooking spray to the griddle as needed.
10. Serve: Serve the pancakes warm with additional berries on top, and perhaps a drizzle of maple syrup.
Strawberry Pie:

Ingredients:

1 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
1/2 cup unsalted butter, chilled and cut into small pieces
1/4 cup granulated sugar
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon salt
3 to 4 tablespoons ice water
4 cups fresh strawberries, hulled and halved (or quartered if large)
1 cup granulated sugar
3 tablespoons cornstarch
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions:

1. Preheat Oven: Preheat your oven according to the pie crust instructions, usually around 375째F (190째C).
2. Prepare the Crust: If using a store-bought crust, follow the package instructions for pre-baking or use a pre-baked crust if preferred.
3. Prepare Strawberries: Wash, hull, and halve the fresh strawberries.
4. Make Filling: In a bowl, mix the strawberries with sugar and cornstarch until the berries are coated evenly.
5. Assemble the Pie: Place the strawberry mixture into the pre-baked pie crust.
6. Bake: If the crust requires further baking, follow the instructions. If not, you can skip this step.
7. Chill: Allow the pie to cool, then refrigerate for a few hours to let it set.
8. Serve: Optionally, top with whipped cream before serving.
